Have any set of instructions read to you.



All you need is an Alexa-enabled device.
Step 0: Enable our AlexaForInstructions Skill either from your Alexa app or at the Amazon Store. You can also use your voice.
"Alexa, enable the AlexaForInstructions skill."
Step 1: Upload your instructions to your online account.
Upload Edit Save
Step 2: Open the skill by using your voice.
"Alexa, open AlexaForInstructions."
Step 3: Use a keyword in the instruction set's name to retrieve it.
"Open . . ."
"Open instructions about . . ."
Step 4: Control flow with 'Next, Back, Skip, Skip To, Pause/Stop, Resume, and Save'.
"Alexa, next."
"Alexa, skip this step."
"Alexa, stop and save my place."
